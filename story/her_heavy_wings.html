Her Heavy Wings

Inspirational

A girl, one who had big dreams, bigger than her. She lived her life bearing responsibility like a parent because someone had to, she didn’t know she was born into a hell but she decided to escape it.

At school was where she truly felt free to fly and explore. She needn’t worry about the snakes, her shackles felt lighter. She worked tirelessly for days and nights. Bared the ridicule, kept her head held low in front of the backbiting snakes to have a chance of escaping, a chance to fly the life she dreamt of, just when she had the opportunity to escape and break of her shackles, she was bound to another set again.

The new shackles had a title; marriage. Once she wore that title her life had ended, but she had no say in it even when it was her life. Soon she bore two fruits before she could even contemplate the life of an adult. She resented the life she had to live; but she never blamed her children for it as they were new lives that couldn’t even fathom the dangers of the world. She vowed to protect them and lead them to the right path to fulfill her responsibility.

As time went on, the little ones started to speak, laugh, love and it bought her immense joy, as they were the reasons she continued to live this life. As the little ones started to fly small distances under the guidance of their parents they were prouder than ever, they cherished them dearly.

Yet, there always remained a sadness in her heart and a constant thought in her ever so sharp brain that she too wanted to fly like them; with them, but they were too heavy due to the shackles.
