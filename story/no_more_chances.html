No more chances

Suspense

A marriage of convenience between two powerful families, they were childhood best friends. They decided to stay married for a year and then divorce because they didn’t have feelings for each other. But as time went on the girl fell in love with him for his sarcastic caring nature and honesty. He would treat her like she was his, he showed that he cared for her when she was down with fever or stress and always held her without hesitation.

They always went on dates, and everyone thought they were in love. He was brilliant when it came to the work, but not socializing, while others either avoided him for his personality she adapted instead and always accommodated for him. She always cared for him in return, they were a team, they understood one another perfectly and took control when they needed to, this was the result of the twenty year long relationship, but one day when he saw another, he was mesmerized by her captivating energy, he started pursuing her hiding that he was married, when she found out, she thought there was something wrong with how she behaved to lose her husband, and she tried harder to win him back but it never bore fruit, he was too infatuated by her.

She got closer to the other woman trying to understand where she lacked to make him love her. But, one day when she saw both of them in a compromising situation she broke down silently in the home they shared together. She knew she couldn’t hate her because she was innocent to his games, she tried to dissolve her feeling for him, she decided to move on from her one-sided love as she never had any room in his heart, but she gave him one last chance, to get closure, she confessed to him on the last day of their marriage. She confessed to him on the rooftop of their house with hot chocolate, his favorite, waiting for his reply, he dismissed her feeling saying he would never like her like that and confessed that he liked the other woman instead and asked her to help him win her over.

That is when she realized that he never took things seriously between them and declined his request to help him. He got irritated but let it go thinking she had no experience. She moved out the next day, never telling where she went, only leaving a note telling that the contract was over and she took the compensation discussed and wished him a good life. The divorce was finalized by the judge before dawn and when the families asked why they did this they said it was due to irreconcilable differences and they were officially separated. The girl’s parents were devastated seeing their daughter in such a state, they then minimalized the communications with his company, not cutting them off because she requested it for the company. She decided to try dating and went on a couple of dates but all backfired.

She decided to take night classes on pottery to take her mind off of things, there she met him, a man who could charm anyone with a single smile. She never thought that she would fall for him but he pursued her. He made it clear he was interested and they started dating.

Meanwhile he was falling apart knowing the one he was attracted too didn’t like him back when he confessed, he then remembered that his now ex-wife cared for him dearly but he never cared, he tries to find her to win her back only to be heart broken when he finds her with another man, they kiss each other and witnessing this his blood is boiling and he confronts her on who he is and why is she with him. Her now lover steps in and tells him to back off of his girl and that is when he realizes he let go of the diamond for some shining gold.

He leaves defeated, he eventually marries a girl set by his family and lives a normal life, but time to time he thinks to himself, if he hadn’t let her go.
