The Velvet Quill
Horror
Fantasy

College life story

Romance

We follow a girl who is living her college days peacefully, some people she knows from her past attend her college and they are not able to recognize her, she thinks that no one recognized her because of her dresses, but in reality everyone forgot her existence all because she prayed that she wanted to erase the pain.

Now that she knows what the being did she tries to create an identity for herself where she is loved by all and tries to make friends. She is not sad at all that people forgot her past and when she walks past her bullies and ex-friends she doesn’t feel the slightest remorse, everyone forgot, everyone but one boy.
