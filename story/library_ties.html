Library Ties

Romance

Two Souls bound by a fate that told many things yet none understand. A boy and a girl, Both of them always sat across each other in the library but neither spoke, they always exchanged books like friends would and if one came late they would realize the other was waiting for them. The never knew each other’s names, where they went, who they were. Despite having an indescribable bond they were content with each other’s company. As one was drowning because of the expectations for his status as a young master and the other she who had none to call family. Both longing for affection they desperately needed which they read in books, they shared the longing of love from who they could never get.

They sought comfort in the moments when reading together. Realizing what the other wanted was the same, they unknowingly found it in each other. Understanding the feelings of one another they called it love, not the one in fairy tales where there is a dramatic ending or realization of one’s feelings but the building of a relationship based on understanding and trust and being there for each other when they needed it which was more intense than any love story many told. When they both reached for each other that is when they truly found what they both wanted. A Romance where they found what they found their soulmate, without words but stolen glances and understanding.
